Salon Appointment System

  • Category: Web-Based System
  • Project End Date: January 2022
  • Project Documentation: Salon Appointment System Documentation

This web-based salon appointment system was created with HTML, PHP, JavaScript, and SQL. It features a client, employee, and admin-facing interface that have different functionalities based on their needs. However, its main purpose is to provide a platform where clients can explore the companies' services and book appointments and where employees can manage their appointments and performance.
