Dumaguete COVID-19 Dashboard

  • Category: Data Visualization
  • Project End Date: January 2022
  • Project User Guide: Dumaguete COVID-19 Dashboard User Guide

The main objectives of this project are to visualize the spread of COVID-19 in Dumaguete City and to identify the barangays in Dumaguete City that require the most attention as well as age groups that are most at risk through maps and charts. The data used was taken from the publicly available COVID-19 consensus provided by the Philippine's Department of Health for January 2020 to December 2021.
